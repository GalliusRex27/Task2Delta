Ball Buster!

  1. Use the left and right arrow keys to move the ball buster
  2. A new ball will split into 2 balls of radius and strength half the original
  3. Balls formed due to a split won't split further
  4. Bullet speed increases proportionally till the score hits 10000, then becomes constant
  5. A ball disappears when strength becomes 0 - doing so increases your score by 1000
  6. Once the game is over, click the canvas to play again
